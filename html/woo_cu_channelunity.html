Reactivate Channelunity Features

Extra ChannelUnity plugin features require a live or trial ChannelUnity account.

Please ensure that your ChannelUnity credentials are correctly entered in the ChannelUnity
tab on the Woocommerce->Settings page

Please also log into your ChannelUnity account to check your account status, and ensure any
outstanding invoices are cleared.

If you have recently cleared an invoice, it may take up to 30 minutes for the system to process.
Please wait a few moments, then click into the WooCommerce menu option. You can then click back into
the ChannelUnity menu option to check again.
